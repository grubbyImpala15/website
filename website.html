Process of building my first PC

I built my first PC about three months ago along with my cousin who was also building his second PC. I got all the components within less than a month because I took advange of its prices, which every component had a discount. The process of building my first PC was so stressful. I remember the day I started building which it was on a sunday at 12:pm. My cousin and I ended up finishing building our PCs at 9pm night time. We spent 9 hours figuring out each component, what goes where, reading instructions, watching youtube videos, taking our time, etc. At the end, it was worth it. I was happy with the results of building my first PC. It was a success and we were as careful as possible to not damage any of the components. Specially the CPU, "brain of the computer. Smallest but strongest and most important component of a computer" which it has really small pins that can bent easily.
